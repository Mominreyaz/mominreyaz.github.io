About this blog

Written in
the space
between
disciplines.

The Margin is an independent academic blog exploring ideas at the intersection of economics, philosophy, education, and social science. It is written for curious readers who want more than summaries — who want to sit with difficult questions.

Essays here draw on formal academic literature but are written to be readable by anyone willing to think carefully. No jargon for its own sake. No hedging for the sake of safety. Just honest intellectual engagement with hard problems.

The blog is maintained by a student of commerce and ideas, based in Kashmir, who believes that the best thinking happens at the edges of fields — in the margins where disciplines meet and argue.
